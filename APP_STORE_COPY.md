## App Store Description (iOS)

STRDR is a cadence and speed optimizer built for runners who want to get faster and more efficient.

Set your target cadence, hit start, and run to the beat. STRDR's metronome keeps you locked in with audio cues that work even when your screen is off.

TRAINING MODES
Pick the mode that fits your run:
• Basic — simple metronome at your target cadence
• Terrain — GPS-adaptive cadence that adjusts for hills
• Fartlek — randomized speed play with voice coaching
• Interval — structured work/rest cycles
• Progressive — gradual cadence build over your run

PRE-WORKOUT CHECK-IN
Tell STRDR how you're feeling before you start. Recovering from a race? A bit tired? The app adjusts your workout intensity and cadence targets automatically.

RACE TARGET CALCULATOR
Pick a distance, enter your goal time, and get personalized cadence, pace, and stride length recommendations based on your runner profile.

VOICE COACHING
Real-time coaching cues guide you through structured workouts. Phase changes, motivation, and technique reminders — all hands-free.

RUNNER PROFILE
Your height, weight, experience level, and goals shape every recommendation. STRDR doesn't guess — it calculates.

RUN ANALYSIS
Upload FIT files from Garmin, Wahoo, Polar, Suunto, or any GPS watch. Get cadence zone breakdowns, pace analysis, heart rate data, and elevation profiles.

Works with all major GPS watches. Your data stays on your device.
